About Our Club

Our Mission

The Green Energy Club is dedicated to promoting sustainable energy solutions and environmental consciousness among students and the community.

We conduct various programs on special occasions like Earth Day and Environment Day, organize plantation drives, and work on innovative green technology projects.

Our Achievements

  • Successfully fabricated an E-Bike with sustainable technology
  • Developed a Solar Vehicle prototype
  • Organized multiple plantation drives
  • Conducted awareness programs on Earth Day & Environment Day
  • Promoted green energy solutions across campus
